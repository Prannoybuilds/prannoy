# 🚀 Overview

Supply chains are becoming increasingly complex, making operational decisions more challenging than ever.

While conventional forecasting systems provide predictions, they rarely explain **how reliable those predictions are** or **what actions should be taken when disruptions occur.**

**EnvireX** is an AI-powered decision support platform that combines machine learning, disruption simulation, explainable analytics, and intelligent recommendations into a unified experience.

Rather than simply forecasting future demand, EnvireX empowers users to understand potential risks, evaluate business impact, and make proactive operational decisions.

Predict inventory-related trends using machine learning models trained on historical supply chain data.

Monitor forecasts, business metrics, confidence intervals, and disruption scenarios through an intuitive interface.

### 🔍 Explainable AI

Improve transparency using feature importance and explainability techniques to understand model behavior.

# 🌍 Why EnvireX?

Modern supply chains demand more than accurate forecasts.

Organizations require systems capable of answering:

- How reliable is this prediction?
- What if a disruption occurs tomorrow?
- Which operational strategy minimizes business impact?
- What should decision-makers do next?

EnvireX is designed to bridge this gap by combining predictive analytics with explainable, actionable intelligence.
