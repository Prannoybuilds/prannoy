# рџљЂ Overview

Supply chains are becoming increasingly complex, making operational decisions more challenging than ever.

While conventional forecasting systems provide predictions, they rarely explain **how reliable those predictions are** or **what actions should be taken when disruptions occur.**

**EnvireX** is an AI-powered decision support platform that combines machine learning, disruption simulation, explainable analytics, and intelligent recommendations into a unified experience.

Rather than simply forecasting future demand, EnvireX empowers users to understand potential risks, evaluate business impact, and make proactive operational decisions.
